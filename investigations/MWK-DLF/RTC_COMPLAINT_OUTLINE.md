## 5. Causes of action (each → its basis + the controlling case)
1. **Nullity of the de la Fuente deeds** — sale of land by an agent without written authority to sell
   is void; "negotiate" ≠ "sell". *Civil Code Arts. 1874, 1878, 1879; Cosmic Lumber v. CA (G.R.
   114311); Alcantara v. Nido (G.R. 165133).*
2. **All post-15-Aug-2005 conveyances void** — agency terminated by registered revocation (and by
   Cesar's death). *Arts. 1919–1921.*
3. **Cancellation of the derivative Torrens titles** springing from the void deeds. *PD 1529 §107; Art. 1409.*
4. **Reconveyance to the estate — imprescriptible.** *Art. 1410; Heirs of Tulauan v. Mateo (G.R. 248974).*
5. **Accounting & turnover of all proceeds** against the de la Fuente side. *Art. 1891; Domingo v.
   Domingo (G.R. L-30573).*
6. **Recovery of possession** (accion reivindicatoria) + **damages** + attorney's fees. *Arts. 428, 434, 2199 ff.*

## 7. Procedural decisions counsel must resolve `[counsel]`
1. **Forum-shopping / litis pendentia vs 26-360.** The Balane parcel is already sub judice in MTC
   26-360. Options: (a) **exclude Balane** from the RTC action (sue the *other* transferees only), or
   (b) plan **consolidation**. The verification/certification against forum shopping must disclose 26-360.
2. **Jurisdiction split.** Nullity/reconveyance = incapable of pecuniary estimation → RTC; confirm the
   accion reivindicatoria components don't force a value-based re-analysis.
3. **Prescription framing.** Plead **void → imprescriptible (Art. 1410)**; be ready for the defendants'
   "innocent purchaser for value / 10-year implied-trust (Art. 1456)" defense — rebut via the
   *registered* 2005 revocation (constructive notice, PD 1529 §52; the innocent-purchaser doctrine fails).
4. **Indispensable parties.** Every current registered owner must be a defendant, or the title-cancellation
   fails for non-joinder → **the John/Jane Doe cluster (T-47655/56/57/48336/69404) must be identified
   first.** This is exactly why the cluster-CTC letter is priority-1 in the correspondence directive.
5. **Misjoinder of causes/parties.** Confirm the transferees can be joined in one action (common
   question of law/fact = the void de la Fuente authority) vs. separate suits per lot.
