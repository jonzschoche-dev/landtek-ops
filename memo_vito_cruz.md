## 4. What Allan can now claim (the uncompensated damages)
1. **Reasonable compensation for use and occupation** of the Malate property — *the centerpiece, never awarded.* Measured as fair monthly rental × the occupation period.
2. **Moral damages** — supportable here (unlike a bare ejectment) by the **proven bad faith** and the family's prolonged dispossession of a Manila residence.
3. **Exemplary damages** — the bad-faith purchase with notice + the **contumacious refusal to honor a final judgment** is strong ground.
4. **Attorney's fees & litigation costs of the *renewed* enforcement** (the alias writ, the barangay case, this damages action) — separate from the ₱100K already collected.
5. **Legal interest (6% p.a.)** on the sums.

## 5. The reckoning period (counsel to choose)

- **Aggressive (supportable by the bad-faith finding):** from **his bad-faith entry, 2011** → vacatur (~14 years), since a possessor in bad faith owes the fruits/value of use from the start of bad-faith possession.
