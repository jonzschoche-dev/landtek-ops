LRA CITIZEN'S CHARTER — Register of Deeds service standards (extracted verbatim from the official
Land Registration Authority Citizen's Charter, 2019 1st Edition, published 06 July 2022,
lra.gov.ph/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/LRA-CITIZEN-CHARTER-07062022_.pdf). These are the RA 11032
service standards that bind the Registry of Deeds and are the enforceable basis for records requests.

SERVICE — ISSUANCE OF CERTIFIED TRUE COPY (CTC) OF TITLE (Charter item 27).
- Classification: SIMPLE (PHILARIS / eTitles) / COMPLEX (Converted / manual titles).
- Type: G2C / G2G.  Who may avail: transacting public.
- Requirements: External Request Form (OAICTD.FRM.2016.007, 1 original) stating title number,
  owner's name, property location (province/city/municipality); one valid government photo ID.
- Fees: first 2 pages = PHP 196.97 (LOCAL RD) or PHP 644.97 (outside the local RD), plus
  PHP 38.19 for every succeeding page.
- Processing time: 1 day (PHILARIS/eTitle); 3 days (Converted titles). Per LRA eSerbisyo FAQ, an
  additional 5-7 working days may be required for manually-issued/converted titles for validation of
  the physical Government copy at the concerned Registry of Deeds.
- KEY COUNTER MECHANISM: if the requested title is UNAVAILABLE, the agency action is to "remark
  'No Record' in the [External Request Form] and return it to the client." — i.e., the RD's own
  procedure produces a written no-record notation at the counter.

SERVICE — ISSUANCE OF CERTIFICATION (Charter item 16; also items 17 Certification/Indorsement).
- Classification: SIMPLE.  Type: G2C / G2G.  Who may avail: all.
- Requirements: request form; government ID; documentary stamp; official receipt.
- Fee: Certification fee = PHP 30.00 (plus documentary stamp).
- The Registry issues certifications as to its records (e.g., status, receipt, and — by the same
  authority that produced the 23 June 2025 letter in this matter — non-availability of a record).

GENERAL RA 11032 SERVICE-TIME RULE (RA 11032 §9, as classified per service in the Charter):
- Simple transactions: 3 working days.  Complex: 7 working days.  Highly technical: 20 working days.
- An agency that fails to act within its posted Charter time, or imposes requirements beyond the
  Charter, is subject to RA 11032 §21 (the punishable acts) — the enforcement hook for follow-ups.
