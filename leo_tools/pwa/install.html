Add LandTek to your Home Screen

One tap to open your matters, deadlines, and documents — like an app.

  1. Open your LandTek link in Safari (this page).
  2. Tap the Share ↑ button at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add.
  4. The LandTek icon appears on your Home Screen — tap it anytime to open your workspace.

Your workspace always shows live information, so it needs an internet connection. Your link is private to you — please don’t share it.

LandTek performs property & legal-operations services only, and does not provide legal advice.
