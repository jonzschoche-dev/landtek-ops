## The pitch

You have land and estate matters that stretch back decades — titles, deeds, powers of
attorney, court filings, agency letters, survey plans. The paperwork is scattered across
folders, inboxes, and people's memories. Deadlines move. Documents go missing right when
you need them. And when your lawyer asks "what do we actually have on this," the honest
answer is often "let me look."

**LandTek fixes that.** We put every document, date, and fact about your matters into one
workspace that you and your lawyer can trust. Three things you get from day one:

- **You never miss a deadline.** Every court date, filing window, and follow-up is tracked
  with a running countdown, and you get a plain-language summary every morning of what's
  due and what needs attention.
- **Evidence you can hand your lawyer.** On request we assemble a bound PDF dossier for a
  matter — the story, the documents in order, and where each fact comes from — so your
  counsel walks into the room prepared instead of digging.
- **One place that tells the truth about your matters.** No more "which folder was it in."
  Search everything, see the full title history, know exactly where each matter stands.

**What we are — and are not.** LandTek is a land and mining-services operation that runs
your property records and legal operations: intake, organization, deadline-tracking, and
evidence preparation. **We are not a law firm and we do not give legal advice.** We make
your lawyer faster and better-armed; we do not replace them. You keep your counsel; we make
their job easier and your matters visible.

## How onboarding works

Turnkey, and the same for every client — no custom build.

1. **Intake call (day 1).** We agree on your matters and tier, and you point us at your
   documents (Drive folder, email thread, or a box of scans — we handle the formats).
2. **We load your matters (days 1–5).** Every document is read, indexed, and organized;
   your titles, deeds, and key dates are captured into your private workspace, kept
   completely separate from any other client's.
3. **Your first deliverables (by day 7).** You receive your first daily deadline digest and
   one sample bound-PDF dossier on a matter of your choice — so you see the value before the
   first full month is out.
4. **You're live.** From there it runs: the morning digest arrives, deadlines count down,
   and dossiers come on the cadence your tier includes.

## The honesty guarantee

This is the part that matters most. **Every date and every fact in your workspace is either
grounded in a cited document, or clearly marked as unconfirmed.** The system is built to flag
what it does not know rather than guess — because a confident wrong answer about your
property is worse than an honest "we need to check this."

Here is a real example from this workspace. A testimony date was expected in a live case.
Rather than print it as a settled court hearing, the system showed it as **"planned by
counsel — awaiting the official court notice,"** and refused to assert it as confirmed until
the court's own document arrived. That discipline runs through everything we hand you: if we
say a deadline is real, it is because a document says so, and we'll show you which one.
