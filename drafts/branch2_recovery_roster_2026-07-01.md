## The unifying defect (why these group together)
Every de la Fuente-era conveyance in this branch shares one or more of the same defects, and the
engine to attack them is now fully local/autonomous:
- **Void authority** — negotiate-only SPA, no power to sell (Art. 1874/1878/1879; *Cosmic Lumber*, *Alcantara v. Nido*)
- **Terminated agency** — lapsed ~1995 / registered-revoked 15-Aug-2005 / Cesar's death (Art. 1919–1921)
- **No consideration to the principal** → no ratification; agent must account (Art. 1891; *Domingo v. Domingo*)
- **Imprescriptible** recovery on a void deed (Art. 1410; *Heirs of Tulauan v. Mateo*)
- **RD certifies the underlying deeds are not on file** (doc:353 — 9 PE entries)

Recovery **strength rises the later the deed** (the more clearly past the authority's end).
