## How this was built (provenance discipline)

The authoritative record of every transaction on a Torrens title is the **Memorandum of
Encumbrances printed on the title face itself.** The complete, registrar-certified record is:

- **`doc:39`** — *Certified True Copy of TCT T-4497*, 13 pages, requested 04-Jan-2023 (Ref.
  2023000031), printed by RD Camarines Norte. This is the **master ledger** and the single
  supporting document for every on-title entry below. (Duplicate certified copies on file:
  **`doc:348`** CTC 04-Aug-2023, **`doc:25`** 07-Aug-2023 — identical text length, mutually
  corroborating. Older partial: **`doc:97`** 1990 copy.)
- Where a *separate instrument* (the actual deed/SPA/plan) also exists in the corpus, it is
  linked as a second-level support.

⚠️ **OCR caveat.** doc:39's pre-2000 pages are handwritten and the OCR is degraded. Entry
*existence, instrument type, party names, lot numbers and registration dates* are reliable;
exact **peso considerations, square-meter areas, and Doc/Page/Book notarial references** are
OCR-soft and flagged `[verify-img]` — they must be confirmed against the title image (tile-OCR)
before use in any filing. The post-2010 computerized pages are clean and reliable.
