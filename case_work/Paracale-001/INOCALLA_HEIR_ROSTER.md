> **Purpose:** Rule 79 requires **every heir named, with age + residence, and served.** This expands the *Inocalla Siblings Summary* (sibling level) to the **second generation** (the deceased siblings' children) that the petition needs. **Fill the blanks; verify the flags.**
> **Source:** `Inocalla Siblings Summary.docx` (operator) + corpus. **[V]** = name found in court/corpus docs · **[O]** = operator/summary · **( ? )** = to fill.
> **Rule of thumb:** a **living sibling** is an heir himself and can give an SPA. A **deceased sibling's** share belongs to **his/her children** — the late sibling's SPA is **void** (agency dies with the principal), so each child must be **named + served**, and ideally **consent** to Allan.
