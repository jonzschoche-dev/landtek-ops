- **7 de la Fuente transactions confirmed** in this cluster: Balane, Hansol, Nacasa, Pascual Jr., Victa, Ramirez (six deeds of absolute sale) + Vela (via a 2000 confirmation affidavit referencing a 1999 deed).
- **Two SPAs are cited across all of them, and NEITHER authorized a sale:**
