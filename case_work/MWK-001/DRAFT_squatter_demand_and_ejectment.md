## PART C — Procedural notes for counsel
- **Vehicle choice:** use **unlawful detainer (Rule 70, MTC, Summary Procedure)** if occupation was by
  tolerance and suit is filed **within 1 year** of the demand — the fast track. If occupation exceeds one
  year from demand / no tolerance, use **accion publiciana** (RTC or MTC by assessed value). Clean title
  (T-52537 active) also permits **accion reivindicatoria** if ownership itself is put in issue.
- **Jurisdiction:** MTC has exclusive original jurisdiction over forcible entry / unlawful detainer
  regardless of assessed value.
- **Authority:** **Art. 487** lets any one co-owner (Patricia, via SPA) sue for all — so this can be filed
  **now, before the guardianship**, running in parallel.
- **Evidence spine:** TCT + tax dec + **RPT payment trail** (possession in concept of owner) + the demand
  letter + bad-faith-builder facts (no permit — pair with the OBO no-permit certification once obtained).
- **UDHA screen:** confirm defendants are not underprivileged/homeless entitled to relocation; the Gaulits'
  competing tax declaration + capacity to pay indicate they are not shielded. `[verify]`
- **Back-rent:** the "reasonable compensation" prayer is the O3 back-rent lever, folded into the ejectment.
- **Gate:** confirm the occupant identity, the demand/rent/price figures (BPO), and RPT year-span before
  counsel finalizes. **Nothing filed or served without the operator's go.**
